The Vietnamese Dong remains relatively stable against major currencies. USD/VND is trading around 24,650, reflecting a balanced supply-demand dynamic. The central bank continues to manage liquidity through regular interventions.

Global economic conditions and Fed policy decisions continue to influence Vietnamese markets. Investors should monitor international developments, trade policies, and regional geopolitical factors that may impact market performance and currency movements.
